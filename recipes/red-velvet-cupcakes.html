Red Velvet Cupcakes

Description

This a delicious and simple recipe for red velvet cupcakes

These moist and sweet cupcakes have the perfect hint of tanginess and will surely become a staple in your collection of recipes!

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sour cream
  • Milk
  • Red food colouring
  • Vanilla extract

For the frosting:

  • Cream cheese
  • Butter
  • Sour cream
  • Vanilla extract
  • Confectioners' sugar

    • Steps

    1. Mix the dry ingredients
    2. Beat the sugar and butter together and then add the eggs
    3. Add the rest of the wet ingredients and then add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture
    4. Pour the batter into a prepped cupcake tin
    5. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean
    6. Beat the frosting ingredients together until light and fluffy
    7. Allow cupcakes to cool completely and then frost the cupcakes
