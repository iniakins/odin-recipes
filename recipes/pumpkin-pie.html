Pumpkin Pie

Description

This a delicious and simple recipe for a pumpkin pie

This is the perfect Autumn delicacy that will be sure to delight your tastebuds!

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin puree
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 eggs
  • Cinnamon
  • Ground ginger
  • Ground nutmeg
  • Salt
  • Pie crust

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220 degrees Celcius
  2. Mix all ingredients until smooth
  3. Pour into pie crust
  4. Bake at 200 degrees for 15 minutes
  5. Reduce oven temperature to 175 and continue backing until a knife comes out clean
