Lasagna

Description

This is a simple and delicious recipe for lasagna

This cosy and warm staple is a dish you can never go wrong with!

Ingredients

  • Minced meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh parsley
  • Garlic clove
  • Dried basil
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cottage cheese
  • Parmesan
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat with the seasoning, herbs and onions
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles until al dente
  3. Mix cottage cheese, parmesan cheese, eggs, parsley and salt
  4. Assemble the lasagna, beginning with a small layer of the mince sauce, noodles, the cheese mixture, a good portion of the sauce and repeat stating with the noodles. Put a layer of mozzarella on the top
  5. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees Celcius
  6. Cover the lasagna with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and continue to bake until the top is golden brown
